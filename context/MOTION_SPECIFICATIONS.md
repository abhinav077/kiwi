# Kiwi — Motion Specifications

- Screen enter: 180ms fade + 8dp upward translation, standard ease-out.
- Bottom dock selection: 220ms width/shape interpolation.
- Task completion: 240ms checkmark scale/fade with subtle card tint transition.
- Add-task sheet: 280ms upward slide; scrim fades in simultaneously.
- Progress update: 500ms ease-out ring interpolation.
- Assistant confirmation: 180ms card reveal; no bouncing or confetti for routine actions.
- Respect reduced-motion settings and disable decorative movement when requested.
